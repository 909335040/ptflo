A tower defense game demo,created by me and one of my colleague, monsters will reach the four coins in order and get to the final portal. Player can build towers on the map. A* path finding algorithm is used in this demo, coins are used as the goal and towers are used as obstacles. ​​ There are four kinds of towers in this game. Regular towers, towers can stun monsters towers deals more damage and towers slows monsters. Finite State Machine is used to manage the behaviors of the monsters.
